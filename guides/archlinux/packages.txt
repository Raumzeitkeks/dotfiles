############ SETUP ############
###############################

* cups
  - Allow administration
    `sudo gpasswd -a USER sys`
  - Web frontend
    http://localhost:631/
  - Reach printer connected to AVM FritzBox
    printer URL: `socket://ROUTERIP:9100`

* dropbox
  - Login
    `dropbox-cli stop`
    `QT_QPA_PLATFORM=xcb dropbox-cli start`
  - Autostart on login
    `systemctl --user enable dropbox`
